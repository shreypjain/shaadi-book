The watchlist feature uses a join table (`market_watches`) with a composite unique constraint on `(userId, marketId)` and leverages Prisma's `upsert` with the compound unique key (`userId_marketId`) for idempotent watch operations. The `unwatch` uses `deleteMany` instead of `delete` to avoid throwing if the row doesn't exist. List endpoints (`list`, `getById`) attach `isWatching: boolean` by checking the authenticated user's watch rows, falling back to `false` for unauthenticated callers. Purchases auto-watch the market for the buyer.
