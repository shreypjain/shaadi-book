The prediction market uses a fixed-supply LMSR (Logarithmic Market Scoring Rule) model with 100 shares per outcome. Key design decisions: (1) Share calculation uses a closed-form analytical solution derived from LMSR cost function algebra, not binary search. (2) Selling is allowed — users sell shares back to the AMM at the current LMSR price with a 10% fee on proceeds and a 30-minute cooldown after purchase. (3) Each market has a fixed liquidity parameter (b) set at creation based on number of outcomes and max shares per outcome, replacing the old adaptive b formula that grew with time and volume. (4) The charity 10% profit contribution is collected externally via Venmo after the wedding, not deducted in-app.
