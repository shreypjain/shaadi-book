When rendering multi-outcome markets in card/list views, show ALL outcomes rather than truncating to a top-N subset with a '+N more' label. Truncating hides betting options from users and reduces engagement on markets with 3+ outcomes. Use the `outcomeColor(index)` utility from `@/lib/utils` to assign distinct colors to each outcome bar based on its original index in the outcomes array (not the sorted index), ensuring consistent color mapping regardless of sort order.
